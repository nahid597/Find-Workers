My app is not working,What should I do?

In case your app is not working, follow the step below:

Make sure that your phone’s internet connectivity is set in 3G/4G mode. If your app doesn’t work even after following the instructions, UNINSTALL the Find-Worker app and install it again from the Google Play Store.
